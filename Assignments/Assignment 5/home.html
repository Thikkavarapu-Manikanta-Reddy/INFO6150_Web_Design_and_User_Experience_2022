Manage debts between friends


  • Splitter is the easiest way to share bills with friends
  • Three steps to be follwed.
  • Register yourself, create group, add friends and split the expense among friends.








Here's an example


  • Hari, Kiran and Manasa went to restaurant and shopping on weekend together
  • Hari pays for the restaurant bill
  • Kiran pays for the shopping bill
  • Manasa pays for the travel bill




Now who owes what?


  • Hari creates a group
  • Adds friends to that group
  • Puts his entire expenses on restaurant bill which will be splitted among Kiran and Manasa
  • Same functionality will be done by both Kiran and Manasa on their expenses on specific event








Settling up


  • Everyone can then see how much they owe and to whom
  • They can settle up and get on with the fun things in life
